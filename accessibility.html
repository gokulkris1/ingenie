Accessibility

Inclusive design is central to Ingenie experiences

We strive to make our digital experiences accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Our commitment

Ingenie Technologies follows WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and partners with accessibility specialists to continuously improve usability for all users.

Accessibility features

  • Semantic HTML and ARIA attributes for assistive technologies
  • Keyboard navigable experiences across all interactive elements
  • Color contrast ratios that meet or exceed WCAG requirements
  • Captioning and transcripts for multimedia content

Ongoing testing

We perform manual and automated testing, including reviews with assistive technology users, to identify and remediate accessibility issues.

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier while using our site, please contact accessibility@ingenietechnologies.com. We aim to respond within two business days.
