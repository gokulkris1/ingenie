Privacy Policy

How Ingenie Technologies protects your information

We are committed to safeguarding personal data and providing transparency about how we collect, use, and share information.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide via forms, emails, and events, including contact details, company affiliation, and information about your business needs. We also collect technical data such as IP address and device information through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How we use information

We use personal data to respond to inquiries, deliver services, personalize experiences, send communications, and improve our offerings. We may also use aggregated data for research and product development.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our business, and when required by law. These partners are bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

4. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You can also opt out of marketing communications by using unsubscribe links or by contacting us at privacy@ingenietechnologies.com.

6. International transfers

Ingenie Technologies operates globally. When personal data is transferred across borders, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

7. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

8. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, contact us at privacy@ingenietechnologies.com.
